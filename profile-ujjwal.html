About

Ujjwal Chebbi is the Co-Founder & CEO of Skorosys.ai. A mechanical engineer who evolved into an industrial automation and IIoT product leader, Ujjwal spent over a decade building and shipping software-hardware systems at the intersection of manufacturing and technology. From developing and deploying robotic programs at ANCA CNC Machines — one of the world's leading CNC tool manufacturers — to spearheading smart factory automation initiatives, he has deep first-hand experience with the operational pain of machine downtime and inefficient maintenance workflows. He completed his Executive MBA at Masters' Union (2024–25), graduating with a Chairman's Award for All-Round Performance, Dean's List recognition, and winning the VIP Demo Day for Industrial AI startup strategy — the genesis of Skorosys. At Skorosys he leads business development, customer strategy, investor relations, and go-to-market.
