We are a team of industrial automation veterans and AI researchers on a mission to bring predictive intelligence to every factory floor in India.

Our Mission

Solving Manufacturing Problems Through AI

India's industrial backbone — chemical plants, refineries, food factories, pharmaceutical facilities — runs on rotating equipment: pumps, motors, fans, compressors, turbines. When these machines fail unexpectedly, the cost is staggering.

Yet 70% of Indian plants still wait for machines to break before they act. Not because they don't want to do better — but because existing predictive maintenance solutions are either too complex, too expensive, or take months to deploy.

Skorosys was built to change that. We combine edge AI sensors with cloud intelligence into a plug-and-play system any plant engineer can use from day one — without a data scientist on the team.
