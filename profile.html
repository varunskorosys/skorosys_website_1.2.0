About

Varun Chebbi is the Co-Founder & CTO of Skorosys.ai — an Industrial AI company building plug-and-play predictive maintenance solutions for India's process manufacturing sector. A mechanical engineer by training, Varun has spent his career at the intersection of physical systems and intelligent software: from running computational fluid dynamics simulations on IBM Power 9 supercomputers to deploying industrial digital twins at Rockwell Automation, building AI enablement pipelines at TVARIT GmbH, researching cloud robotics at Bosch Rexroth, and developing IIoT applications at Portainer.io. He brings rare end-to-end expertise — from sensor firmware and edge AI to cloud ML orchestration and SaaS product engineering — directly into Skorosys's technology stack.
