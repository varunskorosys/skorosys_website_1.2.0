The Solution

How Skorosys Works

A full-stack predictive maintenance system — from vibration data at the edge to AI-driven failure predictions in your browser.

1

Skoro-Sense Captures

Our Edge AI vibration sensor attaches to any rotating asset — pump, motor, fan, turbine — and continuously captures vibration, temperature, and acoustic data at the source, with on-device preprocessing.

2

AI Engine Predicts

Sensor data streams to our centralised ML orchestration server. Domain-specific models analyse patterns, detect anomalies, and predict failure probability — days or weeks in advance.

3

SkoroBoard Alerts

Plant engineers receive real-time health scores, ranked alerts, and AI-generated maintenance recommendations directly in the SkoroBoard web dashboard — no specialist required.

Asset Digital Twin

A Living Digital Mirror of Every Asset

Each monitored asset gets a continuously-updated digital twin — a virtual model that mirrors real-world operating state. This enables not just failure prediction, but lifecycle management, maintenance scheduling, and performance optimisation.

  • ✓ Real-time synchronisation with physical sensor readings
  • ✓ Historical trend analysis and RUL (remaining useful life) estimation
  • ✓ Centralised ML model orchestration across all plant assets
  • ✓ Integrates with existing SCADA / DCS systems via APIs
